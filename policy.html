Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 31-8-2023

Thank you for choosing Vondera, an app designed to streamline your business operations. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Vondera app and visit our website at www.vondera.app By accessing the App and using our services, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

a) Personal Information:

We may collect personal information from you, such as your name, email address, and contact details, when you register for an account, subscribe to our services, or contact us for support. This information is essential for providing a personalized and effective experience.

b) Usage Information:

To enhance our services, we gather data on how you interact with the App and Website. This includes tracking your actions, preferences, and transactions within the platform.

c) Device Information:

We may collect information about your device, such as device type, operating system, browser type, and unique device identifiers. This data helps us optimize our services for various devices and platforms.

2. How We Use Your Information

a) Providing Services:

We utilize your information to deliver the services you've requested. This includes managing your inventory, tracking sales, generating reports, and facilitating communication within your team.

b) Personalization:

We may use your information to tailor your experience by providing relevant content, recommendations, and features that align with your business needs.

c) Communication:

We may use your contact information to send you updates, newsletters, and promotional materials related to our services. You can opt out of receiving promotional emails at any time.

d) Legal Compliance:

We may use and disclose your information when required by law, legal process, or to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property.

3. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your information. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of your data.

4. Your Choices

You can review, update, or delete your account information within the App's settings. You can also choose not to provide certain information, though this may limit your access to certain features.

5. Terms & Conditions

Please review our Terms & Conditions available on our Website for a comprehensive understanding of your rights and responsibilities when using our App and Website.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at vondera.eg@gmail.com.

About Us

Vondera is a comprehensive mobile app available on App Store and Play Store, designed to streamline e-commerce store management.

It empowers store owners with features like stock tracking, order receipt printing, and sales analytics. With collaborative tools, order and shipping updates, and expense tracking, Vondera simplifies operations and facilitates accurate net profit calculations.
